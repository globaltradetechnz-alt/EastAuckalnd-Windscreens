How to File a Windscreen Insurance Claim

Most comprehensive car insurance policies in New Zealand cover windscreen replacement. The process is usually simple: check your policy, start the claim, choose your repairer, and we handle the rest. Here is what you need to know for the major insurers.

1

Check Your Policy & Excess

Check your policy documents or insurer portal to confirm windscreen cover is included. Note your excess — many comprehensive policies offer reduced or zero excess for windscreen claims.

2

Call Your Insurer

Use the claims line or app to start a windscreen claim. AA: 0800 500 231. State: 0800 734 757. AMI: 0800 100 200. Vero: 0800 837 769. FMG: 0800 366 466. Tower: 0800 808 175.

3

Choose East Auckland Windscreens

Nominate us as your repairer. We are approved by all major NZ insurers and can invoice them directly. Give them your vehicle details and your East Auckland address.

4

We Handle the Rest

We confirm the claim with your insurer, source the correct glass, and book a mobile appointment at your home or work in East Auckland. We fit the glass and invoice your insurer. You only pay your excess if one applies.

Insurers We Work With

We are approved by and regularly invoice all of the following New Zealand insurers:

  • AA Insurance — One of NZ's largest insurers. We handle AA claims daily and invoice them directly.
  • State Insurance — Straightforward claims process. We can start the claim on your behalf or you can call directly.
  • AMI Insurance — Popular for comprehensive cover. We are an approved repairer and bill AMI directly.
  • Vero Insurance — Business and private vehicle cover. We handle Vero claims and all required documentation.
  • FMG Insurance — Specialist rural and vehicle insurance. We work with FMG for farm vehicles, utes and fleet cover.
  • Tower Insurance — Wide range of vehicle policies. We are approved to invoice Tower directly.

What If I Am Not Insured?

If you do not have windscreen cover, we can still help. We give you a firm, fair price up front before any work begins. No hidden costs, no surprises — just a straightforward quote for the correct glass fitted properly.

East Auckland tip: If you live in Howick, Botany, Pakuranga, Bucklands Beach, Half Moon Bay, Highland Park or any East Auckland suburb, we come to you. Mobile fitting means no need to drive with a damaged windscreen.

How Long Does a Claim Take?

Most claims are approved within minutes during business hours. For common vehicles, we can often source the glass and fit it the same day. For rare or specialist glass, we order it immediately and book you in as soon as it arrives — usually within 24–48 hours.

Do I Pay Anything Up Front?

Usually, no. If your claim is approved and we invoice your insurer directly, you only pay your excess when the job is done — and some policies have no excess for windscreen claims. We will confirm this with you before we start.
