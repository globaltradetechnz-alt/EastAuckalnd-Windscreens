Same-Day Windscreen Replacement in Auckland

When same-day replacement is possible, how to book it, and what to expect from our mobile service across East Auckland.

Can You Replace My Windscreen Today?

Often, yes. For common vehicles — such as the Toyota Corolla, Honda Civic, Mazda 3, Ford Ranger, Holden Commodore and similar — we can usually source the correct glass and fit it the same day. The key is calling early and confirming your vehicle details so we can check stock availability immediately.

Our mobile team covers all of East Auckland, including Howick, Botany, Pakuranga, Bucklands Beach, Half Moon Bay, Highland Park, Eastern Beach, Shelly Park, Sunnyhills, Dannemora, Somerville and surrounding suburbs. We come to your home or workplace, so you do not need to drive with a damaged windscreen.

When Is Same-Day Service Possible?

Same-day replacement is most likely when:

  • Your vehicle is common: Glass is readily available from local suppliers for popular makes and models.
  • You call before midday: This gives us time to confirm the claim (if insured), source the glass, and schedule the mobile fitting.
  • The damage is straightforward: A clean crack or smash without complex moulding or sensor issues is faster to complete.
  • It is Monday to Saturday: We operate six days a week. Sunday jobs may require next-day service depending on glass availability.

When Might It Take Longer?

Some situations require us to order glass, which can push the job to next-day or 24–48 hours:

  • Rare or imported vehicles: Specialist glass for European, American or niche Japanese models may need to be ordered.
  • Heated or sensor-equipped windscreens: These require specific glass units with the correct wiring, which may not be in local stock.
  • Large commercial vehicles: Truck and bus glass is often made to order.
  • Weekend or late-day bookings: If you call late in the afternoon, we may book you for the next morning.

How to Book a Same-Day Appointment

The fastest way is to call us directly on 09 576-5555. Have your vehicle make, model, year and registration ready. If you are claiming on insurance, have your policy number handy too. We will:

  1. Check glass availability for your vehicle
  2. Confirm your insurance claim (if applicable)
  3. Book a mobile appointment at your East Auckland location
  4. Arrive with the correct glass and fit it to manufacturer spec

What Happens During the Appointment?

Most windscreen replacements take around two hours. We remove the old glass, prepare the frame, apply the correct urethane bonding, fit the new windscreen, and reinstall any sensors or cameras. We then tell you the safe drive-away time — usually one to two hours after fitting, depending on the adhesive used.

We clean up all old glass and debris before we leave. For insurance jobs, we handle the paperwork and invoice your insurer directly.

ADAS Recalibration and Same-Day Service

If your vehicle has a forward-facing camera for lane-keep assist or autonomous emergency braking, the camera must be recalibrated after the windscreen is replaced. This can sometimes be done same-day, but may require a separate appointment depending on the equipment needed. We will advise you when you call.

East Auckland tip: If you live in Beachlands, Clevedon, Whitford or the easternmost suburbs, call early. We cover these areas but scheduling the mobile van route works best with a morning booking.
